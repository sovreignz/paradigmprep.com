Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment?

Students attend one 90-minute live session per week and should expect about 1-2 hours of independent work.

What if we miss a session?

All sessions are recorded and made available within 24 hours. Students can catch up asynchronously and ask questions in our dedicated support channel.

What's the refund policy?

We offer a full refund up to 7 days before the cohort start date. After that, tuition is non-refundable as seats are very limited.

Are there any prerequisites?

No specific technical skills are required, just a reliable internet connection, a willingness to learn, and proficiency in English.
